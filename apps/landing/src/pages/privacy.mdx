# Privacy

*Last updated: 2026-04-20.*

When you submit the early-access application form, we collect:

- The name and contact information you choose to share.
- The free-text answers you provide about your team and the service you would build on rntme.

We use that information solely to review your application and contact you about early access. The form is hosted by Tally; see Tally's privacy notice for their processing. We do not sell your data or share it with third parties except as required to operate the early-access program.

This site does not set non-essential cookies. If analytics are enabled, they use a privacy-respecting, cookie-less page-view service.

Questions: contact us via the GitHub repository linked in the footer.
